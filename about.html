We’re redesigning how India stays.

StayNest is a concept platform inspired by Airbnb and 99acres — focused on beautiful UI and a seamless booking experience.

For guests

Discover curated homes, verified hosts, transparent pricing, and stays tailored to work, travel, or long-term living.

For hosts

Showcase properties with rich visuals, smart pricing hints, and dashboards that keep you on top of every booking.

For designers & devs

This demo exists to show component-based design, smooth animations, and frontend best practices without a backend.

This is a frontend-only concept project. No backend, payments, or real listings.
